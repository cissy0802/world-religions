World ReligionsWORLD RELIGIONS

A neutral, comparative tour of humanity's major faith traditions. Each issue profiles one tradition — its history, distribution today, belief system, and what its adherents actually practice — no preaching, no ranking, no verdicts.

Four-dimension card: one card per tradition covering [Origins & History], [Distribution Today · real numbers], [Core Beliefs], and [Principles & Practice], plus common misconceptions and cross-tradition bridges. Divides labor with Philosophy (systems of thought); complements Buddhism (deep sutra study — not covered here).
